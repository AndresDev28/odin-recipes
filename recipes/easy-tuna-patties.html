Easy Tuna Patties

Description

These tuna patties are delicious dipped in ketchup, mustard, or hot sauce. They are also great served as a hot sandwich.

Make these simple, elegant, and budget-friendly tuna patties with simple ingredients you may already have in your kitchen.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you’ll need to have on hand when you make this recipe for homemade tuna patties:

  • Two eggs lend moisture and help hold the patties together.
  • Lemon juice brightens up the flavor.
  • Use store-bought or homemade Italian-seasoned bread crumbs.
  • For the most delicious results, grate your own Parmesan cheese.
  • You’ll need three cans of tuna for eight patties.
  • A few tablespoons of diced onion gives the tuna patties a bold flavor.
  • Enhance the flavor of the tuna patties with a pinch of black pepper.
  • Fry the tuna patties in a neutral oil, such as vegetable oil.

Directions

  1. Beat eggs and lemon juice in a bowl; stir in bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese to make a paste. Fold in tuna and onion until well combined; season with black pepper.
  2. Use your hands to shape tuna mixture into 8 (1-inch-thick) patties.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry patties in the hot oil until golden brown, about 5 minutes per side.
